== Description ==
Avior SutTax offers sales tax determination web service to retailers. With SutTax WooCommerce Plugin, retailers are able to add accurate sales tax to their invoices.

Avior is tax compliance software provider for tobacco, alcohol, motor fuel, and other industries.

NOTE: The Extension currently only support US based addresses.

= Features =
- Real time tax determination: Real time identification and computation of sales tax for transactions across all states including city, county and local jurisdictions.
- Business Expansion Capability: With the extension you spend less time tracking sales and more time making them. It will support expansion in different jurisdictions across the US by offering tax content for different markets.
- Accurate Reporting: The extension stays up-to-date with the latest tax rates so you do not have to worry about adding the correct amount of tax to your invoices.
- Automatic Updates: Any changes made to an invoice will also trigger an update to the tax amount for the recently changed invoice.
- Added transparency: Identify data sources for visibility on how calculations are performed.
- Generates Automated Tax Returns: With Avior sales tax determination platform you can generate signature ready returns for all your sales tax transactions thereby automating the entire process of tax filing.
- Get exceptional support from our team: Our team of experienced technical staff and customer support is here to help you with whatever sales tax related questions and queries you have and are more than willing to help you find solutions quickly and in an effective way.

== Installation ==

Follow these steps to install the SutTax plugin.

1. Unpack the entire contents of this plugin zip file to your wp-content/plugins/ folder locally.
2. Upload to your site.
3. Navigate to wp-admin/plugins.php on your site (your WP Admin plugin page).
4. Activate this plugin.
5. Configure the options from WooCommerce > Settings > Integration.

OR you can just install it with WordPress by going to Plugins > Add New > and typing this pluginâ€™s name.
